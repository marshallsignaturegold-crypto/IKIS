# Conflict Resolution

## Types

- Content conflict: Disagreement about facts
- Process conflict: Disagreement about process
- Priority conflict: Disagreement about priorities

## Process

1. Direct resolution between parties
2. Escalate to artifact owner
3. Escalate to domain lead
4. Escalate to SSS leadership
5. Document final decision

## Principles

- Prefer explicit documentation
- Record all assumptions
- Flag conflicts, don't hide them
- Preserve uncertainty
