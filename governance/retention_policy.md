# Retention Policy

## Active Period

| Artifact Type | Active Period |
|--------------|---------------|
| SSS initiatives | Life of initiative |
| SSS projects | Life of project |
| Decision records | Permanent |
| Knowledge entries | While current |
| Source notes | Permanent |
| Intelligence briefs | While current |

## Retention Period

| Artifact Type | Retention |
|--------------|-----------|
| SSS initiatives | 7 years post-completion |
| SSS projects | 7 years post-completion |
| Decision records | Permanent |
| Knowledge entries | 3 years after deprecation |
| Source notes | Permanent |
| Intelligence briefs | 1 year after supersession |

## Archival

- Archived artifacts are retained but excluded from active search
- Archival is documented with reason and date
- Archived artifacts can be referenced but not edited
