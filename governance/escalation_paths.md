# Escalation Paths

## Triggers

- Disagreement on artifact validation
- Conflict between artifacts
- Legal or compliance concern
- Ownership dispute
- Urgent decision required

## Levels

| Level | Contact | Response Time |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Artifact owner | 48 hours |
| 2 | Domain lead | 72 hours |
| 3 | SSS leadership | 96 hours |
| 4 | External counsel | As arranged |

## Process

1. Attempt direct resolution
2. Escalate to artifact owner
3. Escalate to domain lead
4. Escalate to SSS leadership
5. Document final decision as decision record
