# Gap Resolution 1.1.3 — BIIPP-2025 Application Deadline (Bihar SEZ)

## Gap Summary
The original Phase 1 research flagged uncertainty over whether BIIPP-2025 applications would still be accepted after the stated March 31, 2026 deadline. This was a potential blocker for the Bihar SEZ node of the SSTP project.

---

## Resolution Status: ✅ RESOLVED — DEADLINE EXTENDED

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Clear Answer** | Yes, BIIPP-2025 applications are still being accepted as of July 2026. |
| **Original Deadline** | March 31, 2026 |
| **Revised Deadline** | December 31, 2026 (or until the new Industrial Investment Promotion Policy, 2026 is officially notified, whichever is earlier) |
| **Extension Date** | July 1, 2026 (Bihar Cabinet approval) |
| **Confidence** | **HIGH** (T1 sources: Patna Press, Times of India, The Hindu, PTI) |

---

## Evidence & Source Citations

### T1 — Official Government / High-Credibility Media
1. **Patna Press (July 2, 2026)** — Reports the Bihar Cabinet approved extension of BIIPP-2025 to December 31, 2026, driven by high investor interest and a proposal from the Industries Department. The policy is extended "or until the new Industrial Investment Promotion Policy, 2026 is officially notified, whichever comes earlier." [Confidence: T1]
2. **Times of India (July 2, 2026)** — Confirms the extension to sustain industrial growth, noting the policy was originally set to expire March 31, 2026, then extended to June 30, 2026, and now to December 31, 2026. [Confidence: T1]
3. **The Hindu (August 26, 2025)** — Original cabinet clearance of BIIPP-2025 with free land, interest subvention up to ₹40 crore, capital subsidy up to 30%, and 100% net SGST reimbursement for 14 years. [Confidence: T1]
4. **PTI / Economic Times (August 26, 2025)** — Confirms free land (up to 25 acres for ₹1,000+ crore investments), EPF/ESI/GST incentives. [Confidence: T1]

### T2 — Policy / Agency Documentation
5. **BIADA Land Allotment and Management Policy, 2026 (May 2026)** — Replaced the 2022 policy; all applications online via BIADA portal; EMD standardized at 2% (MSE) / 5% (Medium/Large); plug-and-play sheds for 5 years (extendable to 15); operations must commence within 90 days. [Confidence: T2]
6. **Central Government Notification (S.O. 4820(E))** — Officially notified the 50.586-hectare Multi-Sector SEZ at Kumarbagh, West Champaran, establishing the local Approval Committee. [Confidence: T1]

### T3 — Analytical / Commentary
7. **Drishti IAS summary** — Policy summary for civil services; notes original BIIPP-2025 features. [Confidence: T3]
8. **Patna Press (July 2, 2026)** — Bihar approved a ₹25,000 crore industrial development plan alongside the extension, signaling continued commitment. [Confidence: T2]

## Alternatives if Deadline Had Passed

| Alternative | Status | Suitability for SSTP |
|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| **Direct SEZ Act application** | Viable — two SEZs approved (Buxar/Nawanagar, West Champaran/Kumarbagh) | **Preferred** for large export-oriented operations; bypasses state-level industrial park limits |
| **BIADA Land Allotment Policy 2026** | Active | Good for smaller units; not ideal for large integrated SEZ |
| **New Industrial Investment Promotion Policy, 2026** | In drafting | May supersede BIIPP-2025; monitor for notification |
| **State-level GST reimbursement + capital subsidy** | Active under BIIPP-2025 | Works in conjunction with SEZ benefits |

---

## Implications for SSTP Project

| Implication | Assessment |
|-------------|------------|
| **Policy vacuum risk** | **Eliminated.** The extension to December 2026 provides a 5-month runway to submit applications. |
| **Incentive stack** | BIIPP-2025 incentives (free land, capital subsidy, interest subvention, SGST reimbursement) can be **layered** with SEZ Act benefits (100% income tax exemption, duty-free imports). |
| **SEZ route preferred** | For a meat processing/export SEZ, the direct SEZ Act route (Kumarbagh or Buxar) is strategically superior because it offers customs-bonded status, single-window clearance, and export-focused infrastructure. |
| **Next milestone** | Submit SEZ developer/unit application (Form-A) to the Development Commissioner, Falta SEZ **before December 31, 2026** to lock in BIIPP-2025 incentives. |

---

## Recommended Action

**PROCEED — with SEZ Act route as primary, BIIPP-2025 as secondary incentive layer.**

1. **Immediate (July–August 2026):** Initiate SEZ developer application (Form-A) for Kumarbagh or Buxar multi-sector SEZ.
2. **Parallel (July–September 2026):** Submit BIIPP-2025 application online via BIADA portal to secure free land and fiscal incentives.
3. **Contingency:** If SEZ approval is delayed, the BIIPP-2025 extension provides a fallback until December 2026.
4. **Monitor:** Track notification of the new "Industrial Investment Promotion Policy, 2026" — it may offer superior terms.
