# FMD-Free Zone Program — Ethiopian Highlands

**Program Start:** August 1, 2026  
**Target Accreditation:** 24–36 months (WOAH official recognition)  
**Jurisdiction:** Ethiopia (Ministry of Agriculture) → World Organisation for Animal Health (WOAH, Paris)  
**Geographic Scope:** Ethiopian highlands (2,500m+ elevation), 5 pilot woredas (districts)  
**Budget:** $2,000,000 over 36 months  
**Impact:** Premium pricing for FMD-free certified livestock (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar)  

---

## 1. WOAH FMD-FREE ZONE PATHWAY

### 1.1 WOAH Requirements (Terrestrial Animal Health Code, Chapter 8.8)

| Requirement | Ethiopia Status | Gap | Timeline |
|-------------|---------------|-----|----------|
| **Vaccination:** ≥80% coverage with high-quality vaccine | 45% coverage (2025) | Need 35% increase | 12 months |
| **Sero-surveillance:** 3ABC-ELISA testing, 95% confidence | Limited capacity (1 lab) | Need 5 regional labs | 18 months |
| **Biosecurity:** Controlled movement, quarantine facilities | Basic (Modjo only) | Need 10 quarantine stations | 18 months |
| **Outbreak response:** 24-hour notification, stamping-out | 72-hour average | Need emergency response teams | 12 months |
| **Zoning:** Defined geographical boundaries, natural barriers | Not defined | Need GIS mapping, fencing | 6 months |
| **Compartmentalization:** FMD-free compartment within infected zone | Not implemented | Need 5 pilot woredas | 12 months |
| **Official endorsement:** Veterinary Services compliance (PVS) | PVS Gap Analysis 2023 | Need PVS follow-up mission | 24 months |

### 1.2 3ABC-ELISA Testing Protocol

**Principle:** Differentiate infected from vaccinated animals (DIVA). The 3ABC-ELISA detects antibodies to non-structural proteins (NSPs) present in live virus but NOT in high-quality vaccines.

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|-------------|
| Test sensitivity | ≥95% |
| Test specificity | ≥99% |
| Sample size per woreda | 400 animals (95% confidence, 5% prevalence) |
| Testing frequency | Quarterly (4x/year) |
| Laboratory capacity | 5 regional labs × 1,000 samples/month = 5,000/month |
| Annual testing volume | 60,000 samples |
| Cost per sample | $8 (reagents + labor + transport) |
| Annual testing cost | $480,000 |
