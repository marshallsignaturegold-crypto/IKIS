## 3. KEY ENVIRONMENTAL CONCERNS & MITIGATION

| Concern | Impact | Mitigation | Cost (USD) |
|---------|--------|------------|------------|
| Aqua Regia effluent (nitric acid, hydrochloric acid) | Water pollution, soil contamination | ETP with neutralization, precipitation, filtration | $45,000 |
| Mercury amalgamation (if used) | Toxic emissions, worker health | Mercury-free process (Miller only) | $0 (design choice) |
| Cyanide (if used in leaching) | Fatal toxicity, groundwater contamination | Cyanide-free process (Aqua Regia only) | $0 (design choice) |
| Particulate emissions (PM2.5, PM10) | Air quality deterioration | Bag filters, scrubbers, CEMS | $35,000 |
| Noise (compressors, ventilation) | Worker health, community disturbance | Acoustic enclosures, buffer zones | $15,000 |
| Solid waste (slag, ash) | Landfill burden | Recycling to cement industry | $5,000 |
| Energy consumption (grid + solar) | Carbon footprint | 30% solar, grid from NTPC (cleaner) | $25,000 |
| Water consumption (process + cooling) | Groundwater depletion | Rainwater harvesting, recycled water | $20,000 |
