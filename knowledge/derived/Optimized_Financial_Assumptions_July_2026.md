## 3. Scenario Inputs for Economic Model

Three primary scenarios have been defined for the transition to the review-ready economic model:

### Scenario 1: Base Case (AISTA Alignment)
*   **Somaliland Royalty**: 4.0%
*   **India Gold Duty**: 15.0% (Current)
*   **Volume Growth**: 15% CAGR (Y1-Y5)
*   **Strategic Rationale**: Successful execution of AISTA bilateral framework and BEZ Phase 2.

### Scenario 2: High Yield (Enhanced Incentives)
*   **Somaliland Royalty**: 3.0% (Negotiated reduction)
*   **India Gold Duty**: 10.0% (Proposed Bihar SEZ exemption)
*   **Volume Growth**: 25% CAGR (Y1-Y5)
*   **Strategic Rationale**: BIBC secures special economic zone status; US recognition of Somaliland accelerates.

### Scenario 3: Risk-Adjusted (Operational Delay)
*   **Somaliland Royalty**: 5.0%
*   **India Gold Duty**: 15.0%
*   **Volume Growth**: 5% CAGR (Y1-Y5)
*   **Strategic Rationale**: Delays in BEZ infrastructure; continued high import duties in India; regional instability.
