> **Comprehensive mapping of institutional capital sources for the SSTP three-pillar ecosystem, with specific focus on AISTA corridor infrastructure finance and Corleone Enterprise growth capital. ## 1. INVESTOR LANDSCAPE OVERVIEW

The SSTP ecosystem requires capital across multiple layers, instruments, and risk profiles. The three-pillar architecture demands differentiated capital sources:

| Pillar | Capital Need | Stage | Typical Instrument | Target Sources |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A: SSS Firm** | $5M-$15M | Seed-Series A | Equity, advisory fees | Family offices, HNWIs, strategic partners |
| **B: AISTA Corridor** | $225M | Project finance | Senior debt, mezzanine, equity | DFIs, ECAs, MIGA, PRI, infrastructure funds |
| **C: Corleone Enterprise** | $4M-$100M | Seed-IPO | Equity, convertible, debt | PE, VC, family offices, impact funds, halal investors |

**Total Capital Requirement (Y0-Y7):** ~$350M across all pillars, with phased deployment aligned to milestones and regulatory clearance.

| **Musharakah (Joint Venture)** | Joint capital contribution; profit/loss sharing; joint management | Somaliland SPV; AISTA corridor JV | Islamic DFIs, sovereign wealth, Islamic PE |
| **Ijara (Lease)** | Asset lease with purchase option; rental payments | Equipment financing; facility lease | Islamic banks, leasing companies |
| **Murabaha (Cost-Plus)** | Cost-plus markup; deferred payment; no interest | Trade finance; working capital | Islamic banks, trade finance institutions |
| **Wakala (Agency)** | Agent manages investment; fee-based; no guarantee | Advisory; fund management; investment mandate | Islamic banks, asset managers |
| **Hybrid Structures** | Combination of above; structured to meet sharia requirements | Complex transactions; multi-instrument | Sharia advisory board; Islamic structuring experts |

### 8.2 Sharia Advisory Board

**Recommendation:** Establish an independent Sharia Advisory Board for all Islamic finance transactions:
- 3-5 members with recognized Islamic finance credentials (AAOIFI, CIBAFI, etc.)
- Annual sharia audit of all transactions
- Fatwa issuance for new structures
- Certification of sharia compliance for sukuk, mudarabah, musharakah
- Oversight of zakat calculation and distribution
