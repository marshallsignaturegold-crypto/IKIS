## 1. Executive Summary

This playbook governs the end-to-end supply chain and manufacturing operations for **Corleone Products**, the CPG subsidiary of **Corleone Enterprise** within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar C**. Corleone Products manages a **16-SKU portfolio** across four categories, manufactured via co-manufacturing partners in India, UAE, and Somaliland, and distributed through wholesale (franchise network), DTC e-commerce, and third-party retail channels.

### 6.2 Expiry Management Protocol

1. **FEFO (First Expired, First Out):** All warehouses and distribution centers operate on FEFO, not FIFO.
2. **Expiry Alert System:** ERP auto-alerts at 180 days, 90 days, 60 days, and 30 days before expiry.
3. **Markdown Protocol:**
   - 90 days: 10% discount to wholesale/franchise customers
   - 60 days: 20% discount + DTC promotion
   - 30 days: 50% discount + donation to food banks (where permitted)
   - <7 days: Dispose per HACCP waste protocol; document and photograph
4. **Monthly Expiries Report:** Submitted to CFO and Supply Chain Director by the 5th of each month.
5. **Target:** <2% of production volume disposed due to expiry.

## 9. Product Development (NPD)

### 9.1 NPD Process (Stage-Gate)

```
[Stage 0: Idea] → [Stage 1: Concept] → [Stage 2: Feasibility] → [Stage 3: Development] → [Stage 4: Validation] → [Stage 5: Launch] → [Stage 6: Post-Launch]
      ↓                 ↓                   ↓                    ↓                   ↓                  ↓                ↓
  [Gate 0]          [Gate 1]            [Gate 2]             [Gate 3]            [Gate 4]           [Gate 5]         [Gate 6]
```

| Stage | Activities | Duration | Gate Criteria | Investment |
|-------|------------|----------|---------------|------------|
| 0. Idea | Market scan, consumer insight, competitive analysis | 2-4 weeks | Idea aligned with brand and AISTA capabilities | <$5K |
| 1. Concept | Concept testing, pricing, volume forecast, margin analysis | 4-6 weeks | >60% concept acceptance; margin >35% | $5-15K |
| 2. Feasibility | Co-man selection, ingredient sourcing, regulatory check, capex | 6-8 weeks | Co-man qualified; supply chain viable; regulatory clear | $15-50K |
| 3. Development | Recipe formulation, pilot batch, packaging design, label approval | 8-12 weeks | Sensory >7.5/9; shelf life meets target; cost <budget | $50-150K |
| 4. Validation | Consumer testing, production trial, commercial scale-up, trade feedback | 6-10 weeks | >70% purchase intent; production yield >95%; QA passed | $150-300K |
| 5. Launch | Marketing, production ramp, distribution, monitoring | 4-6 weeks | Launch plan approved; inventory in channel | $300-500K |
| 6. Post-Launch | 90-day review, volume vs. forecast, margin analysis, go/no-go | 12 weeks | Volume >80% of forecast; margin >target; no quality issues | Ongoing |

### 9.2 Shelf-Life Testing Protocol

| Test Type | Method | Duration | Pass Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|---------------|
| Accelerated shelf-life test | 37°C, 75% RH, ASLT formula | 3 months | Equivalent to 12 months at ambient; no sensory decline |
| Real-time shelf-life test | Ambient storage, periodic testing | 12-24 months | Meets label claim; no microbial growth; sensory >7.0 |
| Challenge study | Inoculation with spoilage organisms | 4-6 weeks | No growth under defined conditions |
| Packaging migration test | Simulant contact, GC-MS | 4 weeks | Below migration limits per regulation |

### 9.3 Consumer Testing

| Test | Sample Size | Method | Pass Criteria |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------|
| Concept test | n=200 | Online survey, monadic | >60% top-2-box interest |
| Blind taste test | n=150 | CLT (central location), 9-point hedonic | >7.0/9.0 mean; >70% would buy |
| Home-use test | n=100 | 2-week in-home, diary | >75% repurchase intent; <10% complaints |
