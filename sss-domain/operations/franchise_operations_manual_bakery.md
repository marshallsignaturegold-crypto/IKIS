## 1. Executive Summary

This manual governs the operations of **Vito Corleone Bakery & Cafe** ("VCBC"), a franchise subsidiary of **Corleone Enterprise** operating within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar C**. VCBC operates artisan bakery and premium cafe units across the AISTA corridor and affiliated markets, featuring freshly baked goods, premium coffee, and integrated CPG shelf retail. By Year 5 (Y5), VCBC targets **60 operating units** across Saudi Arabia, UAE, India, and Kenya, supported by AISTA logistics and cross-subsidized inputs from Corleone Products.

### 2.3 Prohibited Site Types

- ❌ Locations without morning foot traffic (bakery/cafe is morning-dependent)
- ❌ Shared facilities with non-halal meat or pork processing
- ❌ Sites requiring >5 months for construction permitting
- ❌ Locations with prior food safety enforcement actions
- ❌ Sites with inadequate electrical capacity for espresso equipment (minimum 3-phase, 60A)

## 5. Daily Operations

### 5.1 Opening Procedures (Manager/Head Baker Checklist)

**Time: 3 hours before service (bakery production begins at 05:00)**

- [ ] Manager arrives; disarm security; inspect facility
- [ ] Head Baker arrives; begin oven preheat (convection: 180°C; deck: 220°C)
- [ ] Check cooler temperatures; verify dairy, eggs, butter within expiry
- [ ] Review previous day's sales, labor, food cost flash report
- [ ] Verify safe cash count matches POS close report
- [ ] Check voicemail/email for complaints or corporate communications
- [ ] Espresso machine: backflush, purge, dial-in grind and dose (target: 18g in, 36g out, 25-30 sec)
- [ ] Check water filtration system pressure and filter status
- [ ] Bakery: review production plan against par levels and 86 items
- [ ] Pastry display case: clean, arrange, set temperature (+2°C)
- [ ] CPG shelf: restock, check expiry dates, rotate FIFO
- [ ] Pre-shift meeting (15 min, 06:30 and 11:00): specials, 86 items, promotions, safety
- [ ] Activate POS, KDS, digital menu boards
- [ ] Open dining room: set tables, music, lighting, temperature
- [ ] **SIGN OFF:** Manager initials and timestamps opening log

### 5.2 Closing Procedures (Manager Checklist)

**Time: 45 minutes after last guest**

- [ ] Final guest served; espresso machine backflush and purge
- [ ] All perishables covered, dated, stored at proper temperatures
- [ ] Bakery case: cover and refrigerate overnight items; discard day-old pastries per HACCP
- [ ] Convection oven cleaned and powered down
- [ ] All surfaces sanitized (food contact: 200ppm; non-food: 400ppm)
- [ ] Floors swept, mopped, dried
- [ ] Restrooms cleaned and restocked
- [ ] Garbage and recycling removed
- [ ] Dishwasher complete; dishes stored dry
- [ ] CPG shelf: facing, inventory count, reorder flags
- [ ] POS end-of-day: close registers, reconcile cash + credit
- [ ] Deposit prepared; safe secured; alarm armed
- [ ] Security review
- [ ] **SIGN OFF:** Manager initials and timestamps closing log

#### CPG Shelf Integration Protocol

1. **Shelf Space:** Minimum 12 linear feet of dedicated CPG shelf space per unit (or 6-shelf end-cap if co-located with grocery partner).
2. **Product Mix:** 80% Corleone Products SKUs (cookies, crackers, spreads, mixes); 20% complementary third-party (premium tea, honey, jams).
3. **Pricing:** CPG items priced at MSRP or 5% below grocery competitor (cross-subsidy enables margin).
4. **Inventory Turn:** Target 4x/month. Items with <2x/month turn flagged for discontinuation.
5. **Restock:** Daily facing check; weekly full inventory count; reorder via AISTA portal when <25% of shelf capacity remains.
6. **Visual Merchandising:** Brand-consistent signage, halal certification badge, "Made with AISTA Ingredients" callout.
