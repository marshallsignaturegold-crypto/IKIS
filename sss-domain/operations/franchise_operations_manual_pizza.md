## 1. Executive Summary

This manual governs the operations of **Don Vito's Pizza Oven** ("DVP"), a franchise subsidiary of **Corleone Enterprise** operating within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar C**. DVP operates wood-fired, halal-certified pizza restaurants across the AISTA corridor and affiliated markets. By Year 5 (Y5), DVP targets **80 operating units** across Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, India, Egypt, and Kenya, supported by AISTA logistics and cross-subsidized inputs from Corleone Products and Corleone Halal Meats.

## 5. Daily Operations

### 5.1 Opening Procedures (Manager Checklist)

**Time: 2 hours before service**

- [ ] Manager arrives, disarms security, inspects facility for overnight issues
- [ ] Check oven temperature log: ensure oven is preheating to 450°C (842°F) floor temp
- [ ] Review previous day's sales, labor, food cost flash report
- [ ] Verify safe cash count matches POS close report
- [ ] Check voicemail/email for customer complaints or corporate communications
- [ ] Inspect walk-in cooler and freezer temperatures (log readings)
- [ ] Verify all halal meat deliveries are sealed, labeled, and within expiry dates
- [ ] Inspect dry storage for pest activity, cleanliness, FIFO compliance
- [ ] Check restroom cleanliness and supply levels
- [ ] Ensure fire suppression system is armed and operational
- [ ] Confirm all staff scheduled are present or arrange coverage
- [ ] Brief pre-shift meeting (15 min): specials, 86 items, promotions, safety focus
- [ ] Post daily prep list and par levels
- [ ] Activate POS and KDS systems; test printers
- [ ] Open dining room: set tables, music, lighting, temperature
- [ ] **SIGN OFF:** Manager initials and timestamps opening log

### 5.2 Closing Procedures (Manager Checklist)

**Time: 1 hour after last guest**

- [ ] Final guest served; kitchen deep clean begins
- [ ] All perishable food items covered, dated, and returned to cooler
- [ ] Dough trays covered and stored at 2-4°C
- [ ] Wood-fired oven banked and secured; ash disposal log updated
- [ ] All surfaces sanitized (food contact: sanitizer 200ppm; non-food: 400ppm)
- [ ] Floors swept, mopped, and dried
- [ ] Restrooms cleaned and restocked
- [ ] Garbage and recycling removed to dumpster; dumpster area clean
- [ ] Dishwasher run complete; dishes stored dry
- [ ] POS end-of-day run: close out registers, reconcile cash + credit
- [ ] Deposit prepared; safe secured; alarm armed
- [ ] Security cameras reviewed for incidents
- [ ] Lock all doors, windows, and security gates
- [ ] **SIGN OFF:** Manager initials and timestamps closing log

#### Receiving Procedures

1. **Delivery Verification:** Check delivery vehicle temperature log (must be <4°C for refrigerated, <-18°C for frozen).
2. **Seal Inspection:** Verify Corleone Products halal seal is intact; reject if broken.
3. **Quantity Check:** Count and weigh against packing list.
4. **Quality Check:** Inspect color, smell, temperature, and expiry dates. Reject if outside spec.
5. **Temperature Log:** Record receiving temperatures in HACCP log.
6. **Documentation:** Sign delivery receipt; file copy; enter into inventory system.
7. **Storage:** Move to designated cooler/freezer within 15 minutes.
8. **Cross-Subsidy Receiving:** For products receiving cross-subsidized inputs (e.g., halal meat at 15% below wholesale), verify subsidy code on invoice and reconcile in monthly subsidy report.

### 5.4 Food Safety Standards

#### Temperature Critical Limits (HACCP)

| Product / Process | CCP | Critical Limit | Monitoring | Frequency | Corrective Action |
|-------------------|-----|---------------|------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Halal meat receiving | CCP-1 | <4°C (refrigerated), <-18°C (frozen) | Digital probe thermometer | Every delivery | Reject shipment; notify supplier and QC |
| Dough prep | CCP-2 | pH 5.0-5.5; temp <21°C | pH meter, probe thermometer | Each batch | Discard if out of range; adjust water temp |
| Oven bake | CCP-3 | Internal temp >74°C for 15 sec | IR thermometer on cheese surface | Each pizza | Re-bake or discard if under temp |
| Hot hold | CCP-4 | >60°C | Probe thermometer | Every 2 hours | Reheat to 74°C or discard after 4 hours |
| Cold hold | CCP-5 | <4°C | Walk-in thermometer | Continuous (auto-log); manual check 2x/day | Adjust unit; discard if >4°C for >2 hours |
| Sanitizer concentration | CCP-6 | 200 ppm (food contact) | Test strips | Every 4 hours | Remake solution; re-sanitize surfaces |

**⚠️ SAFETY WARNING:** Never stack raw halal meat above ready-to-eat items in cooler. Use color-coded containers: red = raw meat, green = produce, blue = ready-to-eat, yellow = allergen-containing.

### 5.5 Customer Service Standards

**The DVP Guest Experience Promise:**

1. **Greet:** Every guest welcomed within 30 seconds of entry.
2. **Guide:** Menu explained; halal certification mentioned; dietary accommodations offered.
3. **Serve:** Pizza to table within 12 minutes of order (standard menu); wood-fired oven visibility emphasized.
4. **Check:** Manager table touch within 5 minutes of food delivery.
5. **Thank:** Sincere thank-you; loyalty program invitation; invitation to return.

**Service Recovery (LEAP):**
- **L**isten to the complaint fully
- **E**mpathize with the guest's experience
- **A**pologize sincerely (no excuses)
- **P**rovide resolution: replace item + complimentary item + manager follow-up

### 11.4 Cross-Subsidy Receiving Procedures

**Context:** Corleone Products sells to DVP franchisees at **15% below wholesale market price**, enabled by upstream mineral extraction subsidies from Corleone Halal Meats SPV (minerals sold at 33-40% below market into the AISTA corridor). Total Y5 cross-subsidy: **$4.85M**.

**Receiving Protocol:**

1. **Invoice Identification:** All Corleone Products deliveries include a **Subsidy Indicator Code** (e.g., `SUB-PIZZA-15`) on the invoice line item.
2. **Price Verification:** GM verifies the unit price matches the subsidy price list published monthly by Corporate Finance.
3. **Quantity Verification:** Count and weigh as per standard receiving.
4. **Subsidy Log Entry:** Enter the following into the inventory system:
   - Delivery date
   - Invoice number
   - Subsidy code
   - Market price (reference)
   - Subsidized price paid
   - Quantity
   - Subsidy value = (Market Price - Subsidized Price) × Quantity
5. **Monthly Reconciliation:** By the 5th of each month, the GM submits the **Subsidy Reconciliation Report** to Corporate Finance via the ERP portal.
6. **Audit Trail:** All subsidy logs are retained for 7 years per SSTP audit requirements and are subject to internal audit and external auditor review.
7. **Discrepancy Resolution:** If market price reference is disputed, escalate to Supply Chain Director within 48 hours.

**Y5 Cross-Subsidy Per Unit (Pizza):** Approximately $60,000/year per unit in reduced COGS.
