## 1. Executive Summary

This manual governs the operations of **Corleone Halal Meats**, a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) subsidiary of **Corleone Enterprise** operating within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar C**. The SPV is domiciled in the **Berbera Free Zone**, Somaliland, and serves as the **AISTA anchor tenant** for the corridor. It operates a halal slaughterhouse, cold chain logistics, and mineral extraction (salt, gypsum, groundwater) to supply the Corleone Enterprise ecosystem at subsidized rates, enabling downstream cross-subsidy across the franchise and CPG network.

## 3. Slaughterhouse Operations

### 3.1 Facility Specifications

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Location** | Berbera Free Zone, Sector D, Lot 12-18 |
| **Total Area** | 12,000 sq m (building: 4,000 sq m; yards: 8,000 sq m) |
| **Daily Capacity** | 120 cattle OR 300 sheep/goats (mixed: 80 cattle + 150 small ruminants) |
| **Shifts** | Single shift (06:00-14:00), 6 days/week; optional second shift (14:00-22:00) for peak periods |
| **Halal Method** | Hand slaughter (dhabh) by certified Muslim slaughterman; tasmiya recited per animal |
| **Stunning** | Not used (strict halal: animal must be conscious and alive at slaughter) |
| **Bleed Time** | Minimum 3 minutes until complete exsanguination |
| **Chilling** | Carcass immersion chilling: 0-4°C within 2 hours of slaughter |
| **Cold Storage** | 2,500 pallet positions at -18°C; blast freezer: -35°C |
| **Waste Treatment** | Rendering plant (on-site); biogas digester (Y3); wastewater treatment plant (Y1) |
| **Water Treatment** | Reverse osmosis + UV; 50% recycled |

### 3.2 Halal Certification Process

| Step | Requirement | Responsible | Documentation |
|------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| 1. Slaughterman Certification | All slaughtermen certified by recognized Islamic body (e.g., Halal Certification Authority of Somaliland, or UAE ESMA equivalent) | HR + Halal Officer | Certificate, ID badge, annual renewal |
| 2. Daily Tasmiya | Recitation of "Bismillah Allahu Akbar" before each slaughter | Slaughterman | Witnessed by religious inspector |
| 3. Animal Welfare | Animal must be healthy, conscious, standing or lying on right side; knife must be sharp and clean | Veterinary + Religious Inspector | Inspection log |
| 4. Complete Bleeding | Blood fully drained; no signs of incomplete exsanguination | Slaughterman + QA | Visual inspection, batch record |
| 5. Cross-Contamination Prevention | Separate lines for halal vs. non-halal (if any); separate equipment, utensils, storage | Plant Manager | Daily verification log |
| 6. Post-Slaughter Inspection | Carcass inspection for lesions, disease, or contamination | Veterinary Officer | Inspection report per carcass |
| 7. Halal Stamping | Approved carcasses stamped with halal mark + batch code | QA Technician | Stamp log, batch record |
| 8. Certificate Issuance | Halal certificate per batch for export; per carcass for high-value markets | Halal Certification Officer | Certificate registry |

## 5. Mineral Extraction

### 5.1 Salt Evaporation Ponds

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Location** | 15 km east of Berbera, coastal zone (licensed extraction area) |
| **Area** | 50 hectares (Y1); 100 hectares (Y3); 150 hectares (Y5) |
| **Method** | Solar evaporation of seawater; 3-pond cascade (concentration → crystallization → harvesting) |
| **Production Cycle** | 45-60 days (dry season); 90-120 days (wet season) |
| **Yield** | 25 MT/hectare/cycle (dry season) |
| **Quality** | Food-grade: NaCl >99.5%; heavy metals below Codex Alimentarius limits |
| **Harvesting** | Mechanical scraping, washing, centrifugal drying, bagging (25 kg or 1 MT bulk) |
| **Y5 Production** | 2,500 MT |
| **Environmental** | Zero effluent discharge; brine return to sea; no chemical additives |

### 5.2 Gypsum Quarrying

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Location** | 30 km southeast of Berbera (licensed quarry) |
| **Method** | Open-pit quarrying; selective mining; crushing and screening on-site |
| **Y5 Production** | 800 MT |
| **Quality** | Food-grade: CaSO₄·2H₂O >95%; heavy metals <regulatory limits; no asbestos contamination |
| **Processing** | Jaw crusher → hammer mill → vibrating screen (0-2 mm, 2-5 mm, 5-10 mm fractions) |
| **Packaging** | 25 kg paper bags; 1 MT bulk bags |
| **Environmental** | Progressive rehabilitation; dust suppression with water sprays; sediment ponds |

### 5.3 Groundwater Bottling

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Source** | Borehole field, 10 km inland from Berbera; aquifer depth 120-180 m |
| **Capacity** | 5M L/year (Y5) |

| **Quality** | TDS 80-120 mg/L; pH 7.0-7.5; zero pathogens; meets WHO guidelines and local standards |
| **Labeling** | "Corleone Pure Somaliland Water"; mineral composition; AISTA origin badge; halal logo |
