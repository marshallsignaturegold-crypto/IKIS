## 1. Executive Summary

This document presents the consolidated financial model for Corleone Enterprise (CE), the operating subsidiary pillar of the AISTA (AI-Sovereign Trade Architecture) ecosystem. CE operates five wholly-owned subsidiaries: Pizza Oven, Bakery Cafe, Products (CPG), Media, and Halal Meats (Somaliland SPV). The model spans Y0 (seed) through Y10 (post-IPO steady state), with monthly granularity Y1–Y3, quarterly Y4–Y5, and annual Y6–Y10.

All figures reconcile to the SSS Board-approved summary tables. Funding rounds, transfer pricing, and cross-subsidy mechanics are fully integrated.

## 9. Key Formulas

```
Gross Profit = Revenue - COGS
Gross Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue
EBITDA = Gross Profit - Total Operating Expenses
EBITDA Margin = EBITDA / Revenue
EBIT = EBITDA - D&A
EBT = EBIT - Interest Expense
Net Income = EBT - Taxes
Taxes = EBT * Blended Tax Rate (25% from Y4 onward)

Working Capital = AR + Inventory - AP
WC Change = Ending WC - Beginning WC

Cash from Operations = Net Income + D&A + WC Change
Cash from Investing = -Capex - Acquisitions - Intangibles
Cash from Financing = Equity Issuance + Debt Issuance - Debt Repayment - Dividends
Net Change = CFO + CFI + CFF

Unlevered FCF = EBITDA - D&A - Capex - WC Change - Taxes
Levered FCF = Unlevered FCF + Tax Shield

D&A = PP&E * D&A Rate (straight-line, 5-10 year lives)
Interest = Average Debt Balance * Interest Rate
```
