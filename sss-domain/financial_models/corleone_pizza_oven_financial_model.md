## 1. Executive Summary

Pizza Oven is the flagship QSR (Quick-Service Restaurant) subsidiary of Corleone Enterprise, deploying wood-fired artisan pizza units across the AISTA Corridor network. This model details unit-level economics, the 80-unit buildout trajectory, franchisee recruitment curves, royalty streams, and the marketing fund mechanics. ## 9. Key Formulas

```
AUV = Σ(Daypart Revenue) * 365 / 7 (weekly to annual)
Food Cost % = Food & Beverage Cost / Revenue
Unit EBITDA = Revenue - COGS - OpEx (excluding corporate allocation)
Franchise Royalty = Franchise Network Revenue * 5.0%
Marketing Fund = Franchise Network Revenue * 2.0%
Franchise Fee Revenue = Units Opened * $45,000
SSSG = (Current Period Comp Revenue - Prior Period Comp Revenue) / Prior Period Comp Revenue
Network Revenue = (Company Units * AUV) + (Franchise Units * AUV)
Cash-on-Cash = Unit EBITDA / Total Buildout Cost
Payback Period = Buildout Cost / Unit EBITDA
```
