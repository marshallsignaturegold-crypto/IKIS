## 1. Executive Summary

Bakery Cafe is the European-style bakery-cafe subsidiary of Corleone Enterprise, offering artisan bread, pastries, coffee, and light meals. The model presents unit-level economics differentiated from Pizza Oven by lower buildout costs, higher margin profile, and a distinct daypart mix weighted toward morning and afternoon. The 60-unit buildout is staged across AISTA Corridor nodes with emphasis on co-location with Pizza Oven where possible.

## 9. Key Formulas

```
Bakery AUV = Σ(Daypart Revenue) * 365 / 7
Food Cost % = (Food + Beverage COGS) / Revenue
Bakery Unit EBITDA = Revenue - Total COGS - OpEx (excluding corporate)
Franchise Royalty = Franchise Network Revenue * 5.0%
Marketing Fund = Franchise Network Revenue * 2.0%
Franchise Fee = Units Opened * $35,000
Bakery Buildout = $420,000 (vs. Pizza Oven $650,000)
Cash-on-Cash = Unit EBITDA / Buildout Cost
```
