## 3. CROSS-HOLDING EQUITY STRUCTURE

### 3.1 Equity Architecture Overview

The Corleone Enterprise Holdings parent owns varying equity stakes in each subsidiary, with **cross-holding reciprocal equity** creating a unified capital base and enabling transfer pricing for input-cost subsidies.

```
CORLEONE ENTERPRISE HOLDINGS (Parent)
|
+- 100% -> Corleone Products LLC (CPG)
+- 100% -> Vito Corleone Media LLC (Media)
+- 85% -> Don Vito's Pizza Oven LLC (Franchise) [15% ESOP / franchisee pool]
+- 85% -> Vito Corleone Bakery LLC (Franchise) [15% ESOP / franchisee pool]
+- 60% -> Corleone Halal Meats SPV (Somaliland) [40% Somaliland partner / local investors]
|
CROSS-HOLDINGS (Reciprocal Equity):
+- Corleone Halal Meats SPV owns 15% of Corleone Products LLC
+- Corleone Products LLC owns 10% of Don Vito's Pizza Oven LLC
+- Corleone Products LLC owns 10% of Vito Corleone Bakery LLC
+- Vito Corleone Media LLC owns 5% of Don Vito's Pizza Oven LLC
+- Vito Corleone Media LLC owns 5% of Vito Corleone Bakery LLC
+- Don Vito's Pizza Oven LLC owns 8% of Vito Corleone Bakery LLC (brand synergy)
+- Vito Corleone Bakery LLC owns 8% of Don Vito's Pizza Oven LLC (brand synergy)
```

### 3.2 Subsidy Mechanism — How Somaliland Assets Fund Franchise Growth

**The Core Cross-Subsidy Loop:**

1. **Somaliland Assets** (Halal Meats SPV) -> Low-cost raw materials via mineral extraction and halal meat processing
2. **Input Subsidy** -> Transfer pricing at cost + 5% vs. market rate + 40% for imported halal meat
3. **CPG Manufacturing** -> Receives subsidized minerals and halal meat -> Lower sauce/topping/packaging costs -> 42% gross margin (vs. 28% without subsidy)
4. **Franchise Input Subsidy** -> CPG goods sold to Pizza Oven / Bakery at 15% below wholesale
5. **Franchise Network Growth** -> Lower COGS = higher unit-level EBITDA -> faster payback (2.8 years vs. 4.2 years) -> higher recruitment rate
6. **Brand Equity Accumulation** -> Franchise growth -> Media & Licensing value appreciation
7. **Capital Return Loop** -> Media + Franchise profits -> reinvested in Somaliland SPV expansion -> more capacity -> more subsidy -> more growth

### 3.3 Transfer Pricing Framework (Y5)

| Transaction | From | To | Market Price | Transfer Price | Subsidy Rate | Y5 Volume |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Halal meat (frozen, processed) | Halal Meats SPV | Products LLC | $4.20/kg | $2.80/kg | 33% | 180 MT |
| Mineral water (bottled) | Halal Meats SPV | Products LLC | $0.85/bottle | $0.55/bottle | 35% | 2.4M bottles |
| Salt/gypsum (packaging raw) | Halal Meats SPV | Products LLC | $120/ton | $72/ton | 40% | 450 tons |
| Pizza sauces/toppings | Products LLC | Pizza Oven | $12.50/unit | $10.65/unit | 15% | 320K units |
| Bakery mixes/packaging | Products LLC | Bakery | $8.20/unit | $6.97/unit | 15% | 180K units |
| Marketing/content services | Media LLC | Pizza Oven | $2.50/unit | $1.75/unit | 30% | 80 units |
| Marketing/content services | Media LLC | Bakery | $1.80/unit | $1.26/unit | 30% | 60 units |

**Total Y5 Cross-Subsidy Value:** $4.85M (direct input cost reduction across franchise network)

**Franchise Impact:** Equivalent to adding $4.85M of free working capital to franchisee operations, accelerating unit payback by 12-18 months.

## 6. AISTA CORRIDOR INTEGRATION

### 6.1 Somaliland Concessions MoU Alignment

Corleone Halal Meats SPV is the **consumer-facing anchor tenant** of the AISTA Somaliland node, providing:

| AISTA Deliverable | Corleone Halal Meats Role |
|---|---|
| SSTLC Berbera Free Zone occupancy | Anchor tenant (15% of BEZ Zone 3) |
| Mineral concession validation | Active extraction of salt, gypsum, water proves commercial viability |
| Halal certification corridor | First halal-certified export from Somaliland to UAE/Saudi via AISTA |
| Livestock value chain | Demonstrates end-to-end livestock -> meat -> export pipeline |
| PPP concession proof-of-concept | Validates Somaliland government's PPP framework for foreign investors |
| Employment & local impact | 150+ direct jobs, 500+ indirect (herders, logistics, services) |

### 6.2 Concession Security Mechanism

The **Corleone Halal Meats SPV** holds a **25-year concession** for:
- Berbera Free Zone plot (Zone 3, 2.5 hectares)
- Mineral extraction rights (salt, gypsum, groundwater) within 50km radius
- Halal certification authority (recognized by UAE ESMA and Saudi SFDA upon REG-004/REG-005 completion)
- Preferential port access rates through SSTLC

**Concession conditions:**
- Minimum $5M capex in first 3 years
- 30% local Somaliland employment
- Annual production of 500+ MT processed meat by Y3
- Environmental compliance (pending PARIVESH-equivalent Somaliland process)
