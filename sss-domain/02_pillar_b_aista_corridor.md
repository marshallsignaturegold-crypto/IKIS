# Pillar B: African Infrastructure & SafeTrade Alliance / Corridor (AISTA)

> **A sovereign-grade trade corridor moving strategic minerals and halal protein from Horn-of-Africa origination through Gulf trans-shipment and Indian value-add processing, settling in U.S. and Gulf capital markets.**

---

## 1. Overview

Pillar B is the **African Infrastructure & SafeTrade Alliance / Corridor (AISTA)** — a blended-finance trade corridor that:
- Moves **strategic minerals** and **halal protein** from Horn-of-Africa origination
- Transits through **Gulf trans-shipment** nodes
- Processes through **Indian value-add** facilities
- Settles in **U.S. and Gulf capital** markets
