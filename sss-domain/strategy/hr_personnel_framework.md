## 1. Executive Summary

This framework defines the human resources and organizational architecture for **Corleone Enterprise** and its five subsidiaries within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar A (SSS Firm)**. It covers organizational design, headcount planning, compensation, recruitment, training, performance management, and labor relations across all jurisdictions and operational phases (Y1-Y5).
