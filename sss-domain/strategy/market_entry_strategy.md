Corleone Enterprise Holdings enters seven markets across the AISTA Corridor and adjacent regions with a phased, prioritized approach. The strategy balances **market attractiveness** (size, growth, purchasing power) with **operational feasibility** (regulatory complexity, partnership availability, infrastructure quality, AISTA corridor access).
