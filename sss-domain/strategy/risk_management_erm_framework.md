## 1. Executive Summary

This framework establishes the **Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)** system for **Corleone Enterprise** and its five subsidiaries within the **Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP)** under **Pillar A (SSS Firm)**. The ERM framework is designed to identify, assess, mitigate, monitor, and report risks across all jurisdictions, subsidiaries, and operational functions. It supports the Board's fiduciary duty, regulatory compliance, and sustainable value creation.

|-----------|----------------------|-----------|--------------|-----------|------------------|
| **Almost Certain (5)** | Medium (5) | High (10) | High (15) | Critical (20) | Critical (25) |
| **Likely (4)** | Low (4) | Medium (8) | High (12) | High (16) | Critical (20) |
| **Possible (3)** | Low (3) | Medium (6) | Medium (9) | High (12) | High (15) |
| **Unlikely (2)** | Low (2) | Low (4) | Medium (6) | Medium (8) | High (10) |
| **Rare (1)** | Low (1) | Low (2) | Low (3) | Medium (4) | Medium (5) |

**Risk Heat Map:**
- **Critical (15-25):** Immediate board attention; quarterly review; dedicated mitigation plan; escalation within 24 hours of change.
- **High (10-14):** Executive review; monthly monitoring; active mitigation plan; quarterly board reporting.
- **Medium (5-9):** Management review; quarterly monitoring; standard mitigation; annual board reporting.
- **Low (1-4):** Operational monitoring; standard procedures; ad-hoc reporting.
