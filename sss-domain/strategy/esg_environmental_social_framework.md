## 1. Executive Summary

This document establishes the Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) framework for the Sovereign SafeTrade Program (SSTP) Corleone ecosystem. The framework aligns with Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) Standards, Sustainability Accounting Standards Board (SASB) Food & Beverage standard, and Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD). It covers all five Corleone Enterprise subsidiaries ($91M Y5 consolidated revenue), the AISTA Corridor trade infrastructure, and Somaliland SPV operations. The framework supports the Y7 IPO target ($500M+) by establishing investable ESG credentials.
