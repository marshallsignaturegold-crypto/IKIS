## 1. Executive Summary

This matrix catalogs all regulatory requirements applicable to **Corleone Enterprise** and its five subsidiaries across **eight jurisdictions**: Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, India, Kenya, Egypt, Somaliland, and USA/Delaware. It maps each requirement to the subsidiary, deadline, responsible party, status, and risk level. This document is a living compliance register maintained by the Chief Compliance Officer and reviewed quarterly by the Board.

**Compliance Philosophy:** Proactive compliance, not reactive. Zero tolerance for food safety, halal, and AML violations. Transparent engagement with regulators. Continuous improvement via quarterly audits.
