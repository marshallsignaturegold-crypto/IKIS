# Regulatory Assessment Methodology

## Purpose

Standard methodology for assessing regulatory requirements across SSS markets.

## Process

### 1. Jurisdiction Identification

Identify all applicable jurisdictions:
- Entity formation jurisdiction
- Operating jurisdictions
- Customer/end-user jurisdictions
- Investor jurisdictions

### 2. Regulatory Mapping

| Category | Regulations |
|----------|-------------|
| Corporate | Formation, governance, reporting |
| Securities | Offering, registration, disclosure |
| Tax | Income, VAT, withholding, transfer pricing |
| Industry-specific | Franchise, food, financial services |
| Trade | Import/export, sanctions, customs |
| Data | Privacy, security, cross-border |

### 3. Compliance Assessment

For each regulation:
- Applicability to SSS
- Current compliance status
- Gap analysis
- Remediation requirements
- Timeline and cost

### 4. Risk Rating

| Rating | Criteria |
|--------|----------|
| Critical | Non-compliance would stop operations |
| High | Significant penalty or restriction |
| Medium | Moderate penalty or operational impact |
| Low | Minor administrative impact |

## Output

- Regulatory matrix
- Compliance gap analysis
- Remediation roadmap
- Risk register
