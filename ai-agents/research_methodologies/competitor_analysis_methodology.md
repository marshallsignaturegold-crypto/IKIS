# Competitor Analysis Methodology

## Purpose

Standard methodology for analyzing competitive landscape in SSS markets.

## Process

### 1. Market Definition

- Define market boundaries
- Identify market segments
- Determine geographic scope

### 2. Competitor Identification

- Direct competitors (same product/service)
- Indirect competitors (substitutes)
- Potential entrants
- Customer alternatives

### 3. Analysis Framework

| Dimension | Analysis |
|-----------|----------|
| Market Share | Revenue, units, customers |
| Product/Service | Features, quality, pricing |
| Distribution | Channels, coverage, partnerships |
| Marketing | Brand, positioning, spend |
| Financial | Revenue, margin, growth |
| Strengths | Core advantages |
| Weaknesses | Vulnerabilities |

### 4. SSS Positioning

- Differentiation opportunities
- Competitive advantages
- Vulnerability assessment
- Market entry strategy

## Output

- Competitor profile cards
- Positioning map
- Strategic recommendations
- Risk assessment
