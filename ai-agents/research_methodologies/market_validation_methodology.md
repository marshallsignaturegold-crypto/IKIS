# Market Validation Methodology

## Purpose

Standard methodology for validating market claims and data points used in SSS materials.

## Process

### 1. Claim Identification

Identify the specific claim to validate:
- Market size
- Growth rate
- Competitor data
- Pricing information
- Regulatory status

### 2. Source Identification

Find at least 3 independent sources:
- T1: Government/official data
- T2: Academic research
- T3: Industry reports
- T4: Press articles

### 3. Cross-Reference

Compare sources:
- Do they agree? → Confidence increases
- Do they conflict? → Document conflict, explain discrepancy
- Is one an outlier? → Flag potential bias

### 4. Evidence Tier Assignment

| Tier | Criteria | Confidence Boost |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| T1 | Government/official | +2 |
| T2 | Academic/peer-reviewed | +1 |
| T3 | Industry/professional | +0.5 |
| T4 | Press/media | +0 |

### 5. Output

Document:
- Original claim
- Verified value
- Sources with tiers
- Final confidence level
- Any caveats

## Standards

- Never use a single source for high-confidence claims
- Always label external statistics as "external — illustrative"
- Document all conflicts explicitly
